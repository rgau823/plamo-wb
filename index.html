Welcome

Welcome to my resource website to learn more about the plamo hobby. This website is intended for more beginners of the hobby who have been interested in plastic models.

About LockQ

Who are you?

My name is Rocky and I am a plamo enthusiast. I enjoy building plastic models as a hobby. I built this site to demonstrate HTML and CSS.

When did you start building?

My earliest memory is actually a 1/100 No Grade Wing Zero Custom from when I was 6 years old, but I would say I really started building plamo when I was about 14 years old (2008). I stopped building when I entered university, but recently rediscovered my enjoyment in 2022.

What do you like to build?

I primarily started with Gundam model kits (which is probably the most common form). The world of plamo is quite expansive though and I am always interested in building other things. I have expanded my interests to other mecha series and manufacturers and also build some mecha musumes as well.
